heisig: "The color <b>white</b> is a mixture of all the primary colors, both for pigments and for light, as we see when a prism breaks up the rays of the sun. Hence, a single <i>drop</i> of <i>sun</i> spells <b>white</b>."
primit: "As a primitive, this character can either retain its meaning of white or take the more graphic meaning of a white bird or dove. This latter stems from the fact that it appears at the top of the kanji for bird, which we shall get to later (FRAME 1941 鳥). SIMPLE A white ray is a drop of sun."
