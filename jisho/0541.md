## Reading:

### On-Yomi: ゾウ &mdash; Kun-Yomi: ま.す、ま.し、ふ.える、ふ.やす

## Heisig story:

This kanji depicts an <b>increase</b> of <i>soil</i>, multiplying so fast that it literally buries everything in its path.
