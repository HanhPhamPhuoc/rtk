## Reading:

### Examples: 財産 (ざいさん), 私財 (しざい), 文化財 (ぶんかざい)

## Words:

財(ざい): fortune, riches

財源(ざいげん): source of funds, resources, finances

財政(ざいせい): economy, financial affairs

文化財(ぶんかざい): cultural assets, cultural property

財産(ざいさん): property, fortune, assets

財布(さいふ): purse, wallet
