## Heisig V6:

The key word should be taken to mean minute, detailed, or nuanced. Its primitive elements are: <i>thread</i> . . . <i>doth</i>.

## Koohii stories:

1) [<a href="http://kanji.koohii.com/profile/thecite">thecite</a>] 6-1-2010(3): <strong>thorough</strong>: Winds can be thorough enough to blow over houses. Used in the word 几帳面 .

2) [<a href="http://kanji.koohii.com/profile/anragreg">anragreg</a>] 1-3-2010(1): It looks like the wind going over a desk that got in its way. See, it has to go up and over the desk before coming back down again.<strong> Unicode-0x51e0</strong>.

3) [<a href="http://kanji.koohii.com/profile/Wozbot">Wozbot</a>] 7-11-2011(): Simp. Chinese &#039;how many&#039;: this looks like the life line on a heart meter. How many more peaks has this guy got before he flatlines?
