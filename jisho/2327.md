Instead of a <i>water</i> sports event that brings together the best talent, think of a meet of the region's most <i>mediocre</i> athletes, many of whom cannot even tread <i>water</i>. Now try to find a sponsor for the &quot;<b>Pan-</b><i>Mediocre</i> <i>Water</i> Sports Competition.&quot;
