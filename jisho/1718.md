## Heisig story:

Just as the <i>sunshine</i> (Frame 1397) represents the masculine principle in nature (Yang), the <b>shade</b> stands for the feminine principle (Yin). Its elements are: <i>pinnacle</i> . . . <i>clock</i> . . . <i>rising cloud</i>.
