## Reading:

### On-Yomi: ライ &mdash; Kun-Yomi: かみなり

## Heisig story:

The full rumble and roar and terror of <b>thunder</b> is best felt not with your head tucked under your pillow safe in bed, but out in an open <i>rice field</i> where you can get the real feel of the <i>weather</i>.
