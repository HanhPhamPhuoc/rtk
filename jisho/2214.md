# {塞}

## Strokes: 13

<div class="stroke"><img src="../images/E5A19E.png" /></div>

## Reading:

### On-Yomi: ソク、サイ &mdash; Kun-Yomi: ふさ.ぐ、とりで、み.ちる

### Examples: 塞がる (ふさ.がる), 塞ぐ (ふさ.ぐ)

## Words:

塞がる(ふさがる): be plugged up, be shut up

塞ぐ(ふさぐ): stop up, close up, block (up), occupy, fill up, take up, stand in another´s way, plug up, shut up
