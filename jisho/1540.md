安静(あんせい): rest

静止(せいし): stillness, repose, standing still

静的(せいてき): static

静まる(しずまる): calm down, subside, die down, abate, be suppressed

冷静(れいせい): calm, composure, coolness, serenity

静か(しずか): quiet, peaceful
