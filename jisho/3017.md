## Heisig V6:

The sense of this key word is to pull or jerk, as in opening a door or making &quot;ramen&quot; noodles. Its elements: <i>fingers</i> . . . <i>vase</i>.

## Koohii stories:

1) [<a href="http://kanji.koohii.com/profile/chibimizuno">chibimizuno</a>] 16-1-2013(): This character is used as an alternate way to write 希望 きぼう. I am giving it the Japanese keyword <strong>きぼう</strong>. In poor Mexican villages, it&#039;s not <em>uncommon</em> for the people to dream of going <em>north</em> to the US to find better jobs.
