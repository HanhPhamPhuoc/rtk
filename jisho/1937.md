## Heisig story:

<i>Metal</i> . . . <i>little</i> . . . <i>shells</i>. We have saved this character until no win order to draw attention to the visual difference between the <i>owl</i> and <i>little</i>. By now your eyes should be so accustomed to these apparently infinitesimal differences that the point is obvious.
