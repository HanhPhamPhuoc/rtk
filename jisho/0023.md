heisig: As in the character for <i>sparkle</i>, the triplication of a single element in this character indicates "everywhere" or "heaps of." When we think of <b>goods</b> in modern industrial society, we think of what has been mass-produced - that is to say, produced for the "masses" of open <i>mouths</i> waiting like fledglings in a nest to "consume" whatever comes their way.
