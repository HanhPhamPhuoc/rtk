## Heisig V6:

What makes a man a <b>bridegroom</b> is obviously a <i>woman</i> and her dowry, here presented as a small <i>zoo</i> (animals were often used for this purpose in earlier societies) and a <i>month</i> away from it all (the &quot;honey<i>moon</i>&quot;).
